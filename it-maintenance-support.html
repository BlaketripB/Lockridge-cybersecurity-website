IT Maintenance and Support for Small Businesses

Regular maintenance that keeps your technology running predictably — plus responsive support when something needs immediate attention.

What IT maintenance includes

Technology That Runs Reliably, Day After Day

Most IT problems don't appear suddenly — they develop over time through missed updates, growing storage, aging hardware, and accumulated small issues. Regular maintenance prevents the disruptions that hurt productivity and cost money to fix.

Software Updates & Patches

We manage operating system and software updates on a regular schedule — keeping your systems current without disrupting your workday.

Regular maintenance

Performance Optimization

Slow computers cost time. We identify and address performance issues — disk cleanup, startup optimization, resource management — so your team isn't waiting on their machines.

Faster systems

Backup Verification

Backups that aren't tested aren't reliable. We verify your backups regularly so that when you need to recover data, you can — without surprises.

Verified recovery

Remote Support

When something goes wrong, most issues can be resolved remotely and quickly. No waiting for an appointment or driving a laptop somewhere — just fast help when you need it.

Fast response

Antivirus & Security Maintenance

We ensure endpoint protection is current, properly configured, and actually working — not just installed and forgotten.

Active protection

Hardware Assessment

We track the health and age of your devices so you're not surprised by hardware failures. When it's time to replace equipment, you'll know before something stops working.

Lifecycle planning

Breach Prevention & Dark Web Monitoring

24/7 monitoring

Risk Assessments & HIPAA Compliance

A proper HIPAA Security Risk Analysis isn't a checkbox — it's a living document. We perform formal assessments, document your findings, and help you actually fix what we find. Not just report it.

Required by law

Every device that touches patient data is a potential breach point. We encrypt them, lock them down, and make sure that when a device goes missing — and eventually one will — the data doesn't go with it.

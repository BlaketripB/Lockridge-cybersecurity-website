How we approach healthcare IT

What We Do — and Don't — Claim

WHAT WE PROVIDE

We provide IT support, cybersecurity services, and security risk assessments for healthcare practices operating in HIPAA-sensitive environments. We understand how to work in these settings and help you address technology-related risks thoughtfully.

WHAT WE DON'T CLAIM

We don't provide legal advice, guarantee HIPAA compliance, or act as your compliance officer. HIPAA compliance is a shared responsibility involving technology, policies, and operations. We address the technology side of that equation — and help you understand what we find.

PLAIN LANGUAGE

Everything we communicate will be in plain language. No technical reports designed to confuse. No manufactured urgency. If your practice is in good shape, we'll say so.
